VISIONGALLERY PRIVACY POLICY

What kinds of information do we collect and store?

As VisionGallery; to provide better services to all our users, we store basic information -information you provide to us and information we gather from the data you have given us.

1. Personal data - Information You Provide

a. Email - Username

We store the email address/username you provide during the registration for login and recovering your account purposes. Your email address is not being used for marketing purposes; we will not share this information with third-parties or spam!

b. Phone Number

We only store your phone number if you choose to. We have an additional optional security layer where we implement 2-Factor Authentication, therefore require your phone number. Your phone number will not be shared with third parties under any circumstances.

c. Password

We store your password with great caution! VisionGallery only accepts strong passwords and stores them securely by using the latest and most complex encryption algorithms. Once you enter your secure password, it is instantly hashed and salted using the SHA algorithm before being stored on the database. So, we cannot access them in a readable format or share with third parties.

Please note that our website uses TLS/SSL encryption – all data transferred between our service and the users are encrypted.

2. Subsequent Data - Information We Gather

a. Metadata of the photos uploaded

In order to fulfil our features, we extract certain tags of the metadata of the photo you choose to benefit from our services with. We only extract; the “date- time” data to organise your gallery chronologically and provide you with the date-time of the photo when it was taken – instead of when you have uploaded it – and the “geographical” data to present your photos on a world map. We do not extract or store any other metadata, like the operating system or the device information.

b. Data we derive from the uploaded photos using Google Vision API

Our main service is to organise our users’ photos in a certain way and present this to them in an efficient and effective way. The data driven from Google Vision API is the leading factor of our service upon many others (Please see our welcome page to get more detailed information on our services). We store this data on our database to organise your gallery and provide this information for you.

Your subsequent data will be used to derive statistics to present to you, upon your choice. The options you do not wish will not be further calculated or stored with the old results.

Please note that these statistics are only driven to present to you. This information will not be shared with other users or third parties.

How long do we store your data?

Your personal data will be stored as long as you keep your account. The subsequent data will be stored as long as you keep the source image. In the event of a deletion, all data related to the deleted file will be destroyed immediately. If you choose to delete your account, all your personal and subsequent data will be deleted forever immediately.

What kinds of information do we *NOT* collect and store?

As VisionGalley, we are only concerned with the information that is required for us to perform our tasks. Any data that is not related to our features is not collected, extracted, driven or stored. Unlike most services, as VisionGallery, we do not track your location. Likewise, we do not keep a log of your activity or use ML for marketing purposes.

Our Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by the websites that you visit. These text files are used to help identify when you return to a website. Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your computer, when you have gone offline, while session cookies are deleted, as soon as you close your web browser. You are free to decline these cookies by configuring the appropriate web browser settings; however, doing so may negatively impact your experience using our website.

How will we notify you of changes to this Policy?

Any change to our privacy policy will be notified with email notification. We appreciate hearing any comments, questions or concerns that you may have about our policies and practices. Please contact our Privacy Team at privacy.visiongallery@gmail.com.
